Title: Work in Progress Presentation 2

----

Text: The Work in Progress Presentation 2 is the second and key progress presentation at the Interim-stage of the Design Deliverables design and execution process. It is to be presented live to the class. Students should include samples of project elements at their various stages of completion.

The presentation will be reviewed by the Course Director and evaluated using the following criteria:
— has the student progressed enough at this stage to indicate full control of the project execution process?
— all technical issues have been resolved?
— examples of user testing?
— is there enough creative exploration demonstrated?

Interim Presentation will be graded based on the following criteria:
— 60% Design Effectiveness of project elements at the Interim-stage
— 20% Verbal Presentation
— 20% Visual Presentation
