Title: Work in Progress Presentation 1

----

Text: The Work in Progress Presentation 1 is the first progress presentation of the Design Deliverables design and execution process. It is to be presented via PDF presentation posted to the project blog for all class members to view. Students should include samples of project elements at their various stages of completion.

The presentation will be reviewed by the Course Director and evaluated using the following criteria:
— has the student progressed enough at this stage to indicate full control of the project execution process?
— is there enough creative exploration demonstrated?
— peer assessment?

The Progress Presentation 1 will be assessed based on the following criteria:
— 60% Design Effectiveness of project elements at this stage
— 20% Verbal Presentation
— 20% Visual Presentation
